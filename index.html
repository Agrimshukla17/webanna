Biography of
Swami
Gyananad ji

Parivrajak Swami Gyananand Saraswati was born on Magh Krishna Panchami, Vikram Samvat 2023 A.D. (30 January, 1967) in the district of Azamgarh, U.P. in village Nagpur Karhan town, Mohamamdabad Gohana. Pt. Jagdish Tewari is his father and Smt. Nohara Devi, mother. In his Childhood, he was named as Daya Shankar Tewari. He belongs to a simple reputed Brahman family, and the parents, who gave the child to ritual world, knew little of this miraculous child. Daya Shankar had a religious learning from the childhood. He used to recite Hanuman Chalisa and Ram Charitmanas since class two.
